# Monthly Budget Reporting

## Overview

As a municipal employee responsible for monthly budget reporting, your primary goal is to accurately record and report the municipality's financial transactions on a timely basis. This document outlines the procedures for completing this responsibility.

## Procedures

1. **Accessing Financial Data**
	* Log in to FinanceHub using your username and password.
	* Navigate to the "Budget Reporting" module.
2. **Gathering Required Information**
	* Obtain the current month's financial statements from the accounting department (contact: Linda Park, finance@municipality.gov, x1234).
	* Review the municipality's budget codes and ensure all transactions are correctly coded (budget code manual available on FinanceHub).
3. **Completing Budget Report Form (BRF-1)**
	* Access the BRF-1 form on FinanceHub.
	* Fill in the required information:
		+ Date
		+ Municipality ID (MID)
		+ Department
		+ Funding Source
		+ Expense Category
		+ Amount
4. **Reviewing and Submitting Report**
	* Review the completed BRF-1 form for accuracy.
	* Submit the report to Linda Park (finance@municipality.gov, x1234) by the 15th of each month.

## Handoff Notes

I will be out of the office from February 20th to March 3rd. To ensure continuity, please follow these steps:

1. Complete the monthly budget report as outlined above.
2. Review the BRF-1 form for accuracy before submitting it to Linda Park.
3. If you encounter any issues or have questions, contact me at john.doe@municipality.gov (x5678) or Linda Park (finance@municipality.gov, x1234).
