# Permit Fee Processing Departure Document

## Overview

As the municipal employee responsible for Permit Fee Processing, it is essential to ensure a seamless transition of responsibilities. This document outlines the key procedures, contacts, and systems involved in this role.

## Procedures

1. **Daily Permit Fee Collection**
	* Log in to FinanceHub (Finance > Revenue) to access permit fee data.
	* Review daily reports for new permits and fees collected.
	* Update records as necessary using standard forms (e.g., PF-001: Permit Fee Report).
2. **Payment Processing**
	* Coordinate with the Permit Intake team (res_permit_intake_001) to ensure accurate payment processing.
	* Use the Payment Processing Code (PPC-123) when updating records in FinanceHub.
3. **Monthly Reporting**
	* Generate monthly reports using standard templates (e.g., PF-002: Monthly Permit Fee Report).
	* Review and verify data with Linda Park, Finance Director (linda.park@municipality.gov, x555-1234).

## Shared Resources

* Contacts:
	+ Linda Park, Finance Director (linda.park@municipality.gov, x555-1234)
* Systems:
	+ FinanceHub

## Handoff Notes

As I depart, I want to ensure a smooth transition of responsibilities. Please review the following:

* Ensure all daily reports are up-to-date and accurate.
* Verify payment processing for new permits.
* Review monthly reporting templates and ensure data is accurate.

Common mistakes to avoid:

* Failing to update records in FinanceHub promptly.
* Incorrect use of Payment Processing Codes (PPC-123).
* Inaccurate monthly reporting.

Tips for success:

* Regularly review daily reports for accuracy.
* Coordinate closely with the Permit Intake team.
* Use standard forms and templates when updating records.

For further assistance or clarification, please reach out to Linda Park at linda.park@municipality.gov (x555-1234) or visit the Finance & Administration department.
