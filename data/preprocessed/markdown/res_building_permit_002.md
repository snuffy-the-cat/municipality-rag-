# Residential Building Permit Review Handoff Notes

## Overview

As a new employee in the Permits & Licensing department, you will be responsible for reviewing residential building permit applications. This document outlines the procedures and responsibilities associated with this role.

## Procedures

### Daily Tasks

1. **Review Intake Applications**: Receive applications from intake (res_permit_intake_001) via PermitTrack system.
2. **Verify Application completeness**: Ensure all required documentation is attached to the application, including:
	* Building plans
	* Zoning variance (if applicable)
	* Fire safety review (if applicable)
3. **Check for Compliance**: Verify that the project complies with municipal building codes and zoning regulations. Consult with Sarah Chen (Zoning Manager) if necessary.
4. **Issue Permits**: Approve permits via PermitTrack system, ensuring all requirements are met.

### Common Issues

* Incomplete or missing documentation
* Zoning variances not properly submitted
* Fire safety review not coordinated with Fire Department

## Handoff Notes

To ensure continuity, please:

1. Familiarize yourself with the PermitTrack system and our department's procedures.
2. Reach out to Sarah Chen (Zoning Manager) if you have any questions or concerns regarding zoning variances or compliance.
3. Coordinate with Fire Department via phone (555-5678) for fire safety reviews.

**Tips and Reminders**

* Always verify application completeness before reviewing permits.
* Consult with Sarah Chen if you're unsure about zoning regulations or variances.
* Don't hesitate to reach out to me (john.doe@municipality.gov) or other colleagues in the department for assistance.
