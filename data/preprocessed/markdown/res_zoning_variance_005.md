## Procedures

When handling a Zoning Variance Request, follow these steps:

1.  Receive and review variance requests from applicants.
2.  Verify that the request meets the necessary criteria for variance consideration.
3.  Coordinate with relevant departments (e.g., Public Works) as needed.
4.  Prepare a report summarizing the application, including any supporting documentation.
5.  Schedule a meeting with the Zoning Board to present the application.

### Forms and Templates:

*   Use the standard Zoning Variance Request form (Form #PLN-001).
*   Ensure all required information is completed and attached to the request.

### Codes and Procedures:

*   Consult Springfield Municipal Code Section 14.10 for variance requirements.
*   Adhere to procedure outlined in PermitTrack documentation.
